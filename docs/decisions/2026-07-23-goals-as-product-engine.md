# 2026-07-23 — Goals as the product engine

**Type:** Product  
**Status:** Accepted

## Decision

**Goals** are a primary engine of the product experience: money decisions connect to life outcomes (home, wealth, runway). Goals never *own* money — progress is derived from transactions and accounts.

## Why

- Independent professionals care about destinations, not only ledgers
- Keeps the source of truth clean (transactions) while motivation stays goal-led
