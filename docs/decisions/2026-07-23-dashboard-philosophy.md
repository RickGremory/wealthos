# 2026-07-23 — Dashboard philosophy

**Type:** Product  
**Status:** Accepted

## Decision

The **Dashboard** is the center of the product. Users open WealthOS to answer questions, not to record expenses as the primary job.

## Why

- Clarity over bookkeeping theater
- Every feature should improve the user’s financial dashboard
- Aligns with goals-before-reports product principles
