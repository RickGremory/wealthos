# ADR-008

# Dashboard is the Center of the Product

**Status:** Accepted

---

## Decision

Every feature must contribute to improving the user's financial dashboard.

---

## Why

Users do not open WealthOS to record expenses.

They open WealthOS to answer questions.

Examples

- Can I buy a house?
- Am I saving enough?
- How much do I owe?
- Where is my money going?

The dashboard answers those questions.
