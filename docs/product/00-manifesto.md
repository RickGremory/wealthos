# Manifesto

Why WealthOS exists.

## Belief
Personal and family wealth deserves the same clarity, discipline, and tooling that serious operating systems bring to software and organizations.

## Intent
WealthOS helps people understand what they own, what they owe, how money moves, and which decisions compound over time — with records they can trust.

## Non-goals (draft)
- Replace a licensed financial advisor or tax professional
- Promise investment returns
- Hide complexity behind opaque “magic” automation

## Working stance
Clarity over hype. Auditability over shortcuts. Long-term ownership over short-term feature noise.
