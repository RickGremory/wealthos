# WealthOS Manifesto

> We are not building another expense tracker.
> We are building the Financial Operating System for Independent Professionals.

---

## Why WealthOS Exists

Millions of independent professionals earn a good income.

Yet many of them struggle to answer simple questions:

- How much do I really own?
- How much do I owe?
- Am I getting closer to buying a home?
- Can I afford this purchase?
- How much should I reserve for taxes?
- Is my current lifestyle aligned with my long-term goals?

The problem is rarely income.

The problem is visibility.

Most financial applications focus on recording transactions.

WealthOS focuses on helping people make better financial decisions.

---

## Our Mission

Help independent professionals transform income into long-term wealth through clarity, discipline and intelligent guidance.

---

## Our Vision

Become the Financial Operating System for Independent Professionals.

Not an accounting system.

Not a banking application.

Not an investment platform.

A decision-making platform.

---

## What We Believe

We believe that:

- Financial clarity reduces stress.
- Wealth is built through consistency.
- Software should educate, not only record.
- Every financial decision should be connected to a life goal.
- Money is a tool, not the destination.

---

## Non Goals

WealthOS is NOT:

- An ERP
- An accounting system
- A bookkeeping application
- A banking platform
- A stock trading platform
- A replacement for an accountant

Instead, WealthOS complements those tools by helping users understand their complete financial picture.

---

## Design Philosophy

Every screen should answer a question.

Every feature should solve a real problem.

Every decision should move the user closer to their goals.

If a feature does not create clarity, it does not belong in WealthOS.
